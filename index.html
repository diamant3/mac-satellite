Blu Card Requirements

×

DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS

1. Please submit the following BASIC REQUIREMENTS:
* Birth Certificate- (PSA Copy or Certifiec True Copy from the Local Civil Registry (LRC). In the absence of a birth Certificate, PASSPORT (Photo Copy) or PRC I.D. (Photo Copy) may do.
* For female applicant. PSA Copy or Marriage Certificate - for female applicant. PSA copy or certified True Copy from the Local Civil Registry(LCR)
* Latest copy of COMELEC certificate * Latest copy of Barangay Certificate of Residency
* OSCA I.d (Photocopy) * Latest 1 x 1 I.D. Picture (2copies)

2. Additional Requirements: These may be applied if:
* BEDRIDDEN and has NO COMELEC Certificate, submit a photocopy of LATEST MEDICAL CERTIFICATE
* PWD applicant, submit a photo copy of valid PWD ID.
*Senior Citizen applicant whose tenurial status is BOARDER, SHARER, or RENTER, please submit a written certification from house owner and valid ID
* Senior citizen applicant is residing in a condominium, townhouse, condotel, hostel etc, submit a CERTIFICATE OF OWNERSHIP or CERTIFICATE OF TENANCY/LESSEE.
* Makati City Government (MCG) Employee applicant, submit a photocopy of Employee's I.D. Card.

QUALIFICATIONS:
* Must be a Filipino Citizen.
* Must be at least 60 years old.
Sattelite Office in your barangay together with the required documents listed below.
* Must be a current resident of Blu Card I.D. will not be relased if applicant did not attend the orientation. Makati City for the past two (2) years.
* Must be a registered and active voter

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Fill out this form properly and submit to the nearest Makati Action Center (MAC)
2. Attend the Orientation Seminar on Blu Card Program to be scheduled by MSWD.
3. For questions or clarifications, please contact MSWD-Elderly Welfare Section:

Tel. No. 02-8-8701236.

PWD ID

PWD ID Card Requirements

×

Qualifcations and Documentaries

A.Qualifications:
* Must be a Filipino Citizen who is suffering from permanent or long-term disabilities as described in Republic Act 7277.
* Must be registered voter and actual /current resident of the City of makati for the past six months or his/her parents or legal guardian.


B.Applicants must submit the following Documentary Requirements:
* Six(6) copies of latest 1x1 pictures of PWD applicant
* Latest COMELEC Certification(for minor applicants, COMELEC Certification of parents or legal guardian)
* Latest Barangay Certificate of Residency
* Duly Accomplished PWD Profile Sheet
* Medical Certificate with classification of disability
* Duly accomplished Certification of Disability signed by the Barangay Health Center Physician or Private Doctor.
